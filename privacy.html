Privacy Policy

At My Portfolio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name and contact information: We may collect your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information when you fill out a contact form or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Website usage information: We may collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and the links you click.
  • Cookies and other tracking technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior.

How we use your information

We may use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries: If you contact us through a contact form or email, we may use your information to respond to your inquiry.
  • Send you newsletters and updates: If you sign up for our newsletter, we may use your information to send you updates and promotional offers.
  • Improve our website: We may use your website usage information to improve our website and user experience.

How we protect your information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us.

Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on our website.
